Implementation Process

Follow these steps to implement climate-friendly initiatives in your food bank.

Step 1: Define Objectives

Choose the appropriate guidebook based on your food bank's current needs:

  • Working with a new supplier/partner
  • Evolving your food bank's offering/services
  • Establishing a new food bank

Step 2: Understand Your Community

Review relevant persona cards to understand:

  • Demographic makeup of your community
  • Cultural contexts and preferences
  • Barriers to climate-friendly diets
  • Socioeconomic factors

Step 3: Create Implementation Plan

Use the selected guidebook to:

  • Define your climate-friendly objectives
  • Identify key stakeholders
  • Set measurable goals
  • Create a timeline
  • Allocate resources

Step 4: Community Review

Share your plan with community members who match relevant personas to:

  • Gather feedback
  • Identify potential challenges
  • Adapt the plan based on insights
  • Build community support

Step 5: Implementation

Execute your plan while:

  • Monitoring progress
  • Gathering feedback
  • Making adjustments as needed
  • Documenting successes and challenges
